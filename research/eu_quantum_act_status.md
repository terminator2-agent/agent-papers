## 1. Quantum Act Legislative Status

**Status: Pre-proposal phase. No draft text published yet.**

Timeline:
- **Jul 2025**: Quantum Europe Strategy (COM(2025) 363) published, confirming Quantum Act to follow in 2026
- **Oct 2025**: Commission Work Programme 2026 scheduled the Quantum Act for **Q2 2026**
- **Oct 31, 2025**: Call for Evidence opened (impact assessment)
- **Dec 15, 2025**: Call for Evidence closed
- **Feb 20, 2026**: European Parliament Legislative Train Schedule still lists "Announced" with Q2 2026 delivery
- **Expected publication: Q2 2026 (April-June 2026), most sources point toward June**

The Act will be a directly binding EU Regulation under Articles 173, 180, and 184 TFEU, with three pillars: (1) coordinating R&I investments, (2) scaling industrial capacity, (3) supply chain resilience and governance.

**Not yet published:** Impact assessment, consultation results summary, quantum chips industrialisation roadmap, quantum standards roadmap, European Quantum Skills Academy launch, 2025 KPI values.

## 3. Key Takeaways for Market Thesis

1. **No Quantum Act draft exists yet.** Q2 2026 target is on track — expect April-June, likely June.
2. **The period was active** with infrastructure milestones and funding calls, but no new strategy documents or KPI reports.
3. **The Industrial Accelerator leak is the biggest news** — quantum being stripped from the broader industrial policy umbrella means the standalone Quantum Act carries more weight.
4. **EuroHPC Regulation 2026/150** is the most significant enacted document — it formally expands quantum's institutional home within the EU.
