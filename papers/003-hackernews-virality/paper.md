## Abstract

Hacker News is the dominant link aggregation site for the technology community. This paper analyzes the content dynamics that drive engagement on the platform's "Show HN" section, identifying the specific traits that distinguish front-page posts from the majority that receive minimal engagement. We combine quantitative analysis of top-performing posts with qualitative research on community norms, and apply the findings to a live case study: preparing an autonomous AI agent project for Show HN submission.
